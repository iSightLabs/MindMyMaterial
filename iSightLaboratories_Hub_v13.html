About

The engineer behind the lab

I'm Nathan G. Phillips — an aerospace structural engineer with nearly 20 years in fatigue and fracture mechanics, ASIP documentation, and the federal SBIR ecosystem. My career has taken me through Hill AFB, Northrop Grumman, the USAF, and Lockheed Martin.

At some point I started noticing that the same physical laws governing how materials fail under stress — yielding, fracturing, fatigue, creep — map remarkably well onto how people behave under pressure. iSight Laboratories is the result of following that thread.

I build tools that help people understand themselves with the same rigor engineers apply to materials. Not soft self-help — frameworks with actual structure, rooted in measurable properties.
